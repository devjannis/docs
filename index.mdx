# What is Pynqo

Pynqo helps communities like yours stay informed by delivering only the most relevant information to each user—instantly and intelligently.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Real-Time Keyword Alerts" icon="bell" href="https://pynqo.com">
    Users define keywords, and Pynqo instantly notifies them when those are mentioned in monitored channels.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Exclude Irrelevant Terms" icon="filter" href="https://pynqo.com">
    Users can exclude certain words to avoid spammy or off-topic pings.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Blazing Fast Delivery" icon="rocket" href="https://pynqo.com">
    Notifications are sent in just 0.4 ms—perfect for fast-paced markets like crypto or news.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-Platform Expansion" icon="desktop" href="https://pynqo.com">
    A web dashboard and Chrome extension are in the works so users stay updated even outside of Discord.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
